Chilli Potato

Ingredients

refined oil 2 teaspoon
red chilliflakes 1 teaspoon
salt 1 teaspoon
dark soy sauce 1 teaspoon
potato 3 units
tomato 4 units
spring onion 20 g
pepper 0.5 teaspoon
garlic 5 pieces
water 30 ml

A super party dish to make is the Baked Chili Potato Dish. This vegan dish having its origin from the Hakka cuisine is fairly easy to put together and can be made as a side dish, starters or even as finger food. The Indo Chinese cooking has originated from the Chinese settlers in Kolkata, generations ago. The modern Indo Chinese cooking vastly differs from the food found in China. Of late there is not a single city in India that does not have a few Chinese restaurants! The only way to describe the Baked chili potato is mouth-watering. How to make the baked chili potato so that it is a favorite of both the young and old? The answer lies in the sauce that coats the potato wedges. The Baked chili potato wedges are dunked in a spicy, sour and mildly sweet sauce to tickle the palate and make you crave for more. They are then baked in the oven to get a crispy coat and for an extra layer of crunch, the Baked chili potato wedges are tossed in toasted sesame seeds and slivers of green spring onions to bring out another level of deliciousness. The Baked chili potato video is a foolproof plan to make this scrumptious dish. Baked chili potato description would be, crispy on the exterior and soft in the inside, with some nutty flavor from the sesame seeds and a zing of freshness from the chopped green spring onions. Baked chili potato calories are drastically reduced with the elimination of sugar which is substituted by the tomatoes to give mild sweetness to the dish and bake the potato wedges instead of deep-frying. This definitely lowers the calories making it a lot healthier.

Raghav Tandon

Directions

  1. Wash and boil the potato. Once it is boiled, cut it into wedges, add salt and little oil to it and bake it 8-10 minutes at 220 degrees c

  2. Fine chop the garlic, tomato, and green part of the spring onion. Cut the spring onion bulb into a roughly diamond shape and set aside. Take a pan, add a little oil, add half of the chopped garlic to it and saute it for a few minutes. Add black pepper crush along with chili flakes and cook it for 1-2 minutes. Add roughly diamond spring onion bulb saute it well.

  3. Add chopped tomato along with the salt and let it cook till the tomato gets mashy .

  4. Add soya sauce to the cooked tomato, stir well and cook it for another 2 minute.

  5. Take out the mixture in a bowl and cool down the temperature

  6. Once it gets cold take a mixi jar and make a fine puree of it and set aside .

  7. Take a pan, add leftover chopped garlic and saute it till golden color, add sauce to it and mix well with sauteed garlic .

  8. Add roasted potato to the sauce and toss well if the sauce is thick add little water to it and let it cook for 1 minute .

  9. Finish it with the chopped spring onion and serve hot .

Nutrition Info. (per serving)

Make this dish better

Serve the baked chili potato immediately after it's cooked to keep the potatoes moist and crispy. Potatoes can be air-fried for a healthier option. You can use baby potatoes along with the skin, sliced in half. You need to cut the potato wedges to one size to ensure that they cook evenly. For extra crisp baked chili potato wedges, dunk the potato chunks into boiling water for a few minutes to remove the starch. Thus stopping them from going soggy while baking.
